# Digital Rights Management (dDRM)

Elacity's **dDRM** (Decentralized Digital Rights Management) enables sovereign ownership and trading of digital assets without intermediaries.

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Protect

Encrypt your assets so AI cannot scrape them for free.

```
Traditional DRM         →    Elacity dDRM
─────────────────────────────────────────
Central servers              Your hardware
Company controls             You control
Can be revoked               Mathematically yours
```

Your content is **mathematically invisible** without an Access Token.

### 2. Automate

Embed "Rights Logic" that forces AI agents and individuals to pay you instantly.

- No manual invoicing
- No chasing payments
- No payment processors

Smart contracts enforce licensing automatically.

### 3. Settle

Bypass banks and platforms. Revenue hits your wallet in seconds.

- No "Net-30" days
- No intermediaries
- No platform fees (beyond gas)

## How dDRM Differs

| Traditional DRM | Elacity dDRM |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Centralized servers | Decentralized (IPFS + blockchain) |
| Platform controls access | You control access |
| Revenue delayed | Revenue instant |
| Can be revoked | Mathematically enforced |
| Platform takes cut | Direct to your wallet |

## Supported Content Types

- **Video/Audio** - Streaming with encrypted chunks
- **Images** - High-resolution with watermarking
- **Documents** - PDFs, papers, reports
- **Data** - Datasets, APIs, feeds
- **Code** - Algorithms, models, scripts

## Revenue Models

| Model | Description | Use Case |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Pay-per-view | Single access fee | Videos, articles |
| Subscription | Recurring access | Streaming, APIs |
| Royalty splits | % to multiple parties | Collaborations |
| Resale rights | Secondary market | NFT-style trading |

<Callout type="info">
All revenue models are enforced by smart contracts - no trust required.
</Callout>
