# Legal

> **Version 1.0** &nbsp;•&nbsp; **Effective Date:** 27 May 2026 &nbsp;•&nbsp; **Last Updated:** 27 May 2026

These are the documents that govern your use of the **Elacity Exchange** at
[ela.city](https://ela.city). They are written in plain English wherever the law
permits, with surgical legalese only where it is required for enforceability.

If you have a question about anything in these documents, please email
[legal@ela.city](mailto:legal@ela.city) before you accept them.

your digital assets. You are your own bank. Read the [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service)
and the [Risk Disclosures](/legal/risk-disclosures) before connecting a wallet.

## How these documents relate

Every document is **incorporated into the Terms of Service by reference**. That means
when you accept the Terms, you also accept the Privacy Policy, the Acceptable Use
Policy, the Risk Disclosures, and the DMCA Procedure. Together they are the
*Operative Documents*.

Where any document conflicts with another:

- The **Terms of Service** prevail in respect of contractual matters.
- The **Privacy Policy** prevails in respect of data-protection matters.
