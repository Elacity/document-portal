trigger re-acceptance in the welcome consent flow at your next wallet connect.

## How versioning works

- **Version number** (e.g. `1.0`, `1.1`, `2.0`) — increments on every published change.
- **Effective Date** — the date the version takes effect for new acceptances and
  for continued use of the Exchange.
- **Material change** — a change that affects rights, obligations, dispute
  resolution, or risk profile. Triggers re-consent. Typo corrections and
  clarifying edits are not material.
